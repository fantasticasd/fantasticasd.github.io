The Truth Detective

"The Truth Detective" by Tim Harford is a compelling and accessible guide to critical thinking and spotting misinformation in the modern world. Using engaging stories, real-life examples, and insights from economics and statistics, Harford teaches readers how to think clearly, question assumptions, and separate truth from lies in an age of information overload. It’s a practical and entertaining toolkit for anyone who wants to become more rational and discerning in their everyday decisions.
